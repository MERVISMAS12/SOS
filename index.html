Statistical Optimizer for Student

Statistic optimizer for students is a interactive software interface build for students, to optimize their academic performance by keeping track of marks and attendance. It also provides study material. This helps the student in getting good grades, and helps the student to not fall into defaulters list. This can also be a platform for parents, mentors, and teachers to keep track of their child’s performance and attendance. This is a student-based interface where the student will have to enter their information weekly for their own betterment.

Desired score

Every student knows their own capability. SOS provides the student with a feature called desired score. Desired score takes in the target (cgpa) of a student, and their strong and weak subjects, creates a target for the student. The target changes according to the marks the student scores in his/her internal assessment tests. This helps the student in working hard towards their goal and knowing which subject they must work on. It also encourages students to study well and know their capabilities.

Attendance

The main aim of attendance tracker is to ensure that students have track of their attendance so that they do not fall into defaulters list. This is made possible by keeping track of weekly absentees, subject wise. This helps in keeping students informed about their attendance and how much they are lacing per subject. It benefits the student to gain focus in studies. In case a student needs to take a leave because they are sick or any emergency then the student can get to know how percent of attendance he or she has lost.
